Welcome! This is the first post on my new personal site.

I'm Christina, a Partner Solution Architect. I work with partners to design and
build cloud and AI solutions — and I plan to use this space to share what I learn
along the way.

## What to expect

- **Architecture notes** — patterns, trade-offs, and lessons from real projects
- **Cloud & DevOps** — practical tips for shipping reliable software
- **AI in practice** — how I use and reason about modern AI tooling

## Why a static site?

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and hosted for free on
GitHub Pages. It's fast, secure (no server to maintain), and easy to update —
each post is just a Markdown file.

Thanks for stopping by. More soon!
